HOW TO PLAY PIG-DICE-GAME

Step 1: Objective of the Game

To score 100 points before your opponent



Step 2: What Do You Need

A piece of paper Pencil/pen/marker or any writing thing 2 dies that have 6 faces

Step 3: RULES

*2 ones are called snake eyes if you get them you lose your points for the whole game once I lost them at 96 *Roll on every turn *Mark all your points on a piece of paper *Roll 1 or more time until you want to *if you get a one in any of your rolls you get a 0 for that round *Miss a turn if you got Snake eyes
